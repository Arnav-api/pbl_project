Introduction

Web application featuring object identification, condition assessment, and automated product listing. Reduces human effort by providing end-to-end usable products.

Uses CNN-based classification and damage detection to streamline workflows and enhance productivity.

Problem Statement

Manual product listing is time-consuming and inconsistent.
Existing platforms lack automatic damage detection.
No integration of classification + condition assessment.

Literature Review

CNN and YOLOv8 have significantly improved object detection and classification. Platforms like OLX and eBay use tagging but rely heavily on manual input.

Gap: Most systems focus on either classification OR damage detection, not both.
